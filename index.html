About Us

"Learn and Grow with Angela," founded by Angela Demant, offers a transformative solution to the complexities of modern education. Angela recognized the urgent need for change amidst challenges like overcrowded classrooms, rising school violence, and the academic setbacks caused by COVID-19. Her program provides a nurturing environment where each child's needs and safety are prioritized, and learning is personalized to align with family values.

Angela's vision addresses these issues directly by implementing smaller class sizes, ensuring every student receives personalized attention and reducing distractions. The program fosters a supportive atmosphere that minimizes bullying and violence, providing students with a safe space to learn and grow.

Central to Angela's approach is the innovative SPARK Learning System, which allows parents to choose materials that resonate with their family's values. This collaboration between parents and teachers ensures a curriculum that not only meets academic standards but also nurtures well-rounded individuals.

"Learn and Grow with Angela" represents more than just an educational program; it's a movement towards a safer, more personalized learning experience. Angela's vision offers hope for students facing the challenges of modern education, promising a future where every child can thrive academically and personally.
